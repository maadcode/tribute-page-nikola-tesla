About Nikola Tesla

Nikola Tesla, of Serbian ethnicity, was born in the town of Smiljan (currently in Croatia), in the then Austro-Hungarian Empire, and later became an American citizen.

After his demonstration of wireless radio wave communication in 1894 and after his victory in the War of the Currents, he was widely recognized as one of the best electrical engineers in the United States of America.

Inventions and discoveries

  • Wireless transfer of electrical energy using electromagnetic waves.
  • Alternating current generator.
  • Bulb without filament or fluorescent lamp.
  • Electrotherapy or diagnostic devices, especially a single electrode X-ray generator.
  • Paddleless turbine, operated by fluid friction
  • Tesla coil
